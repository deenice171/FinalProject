BRIDAL PREVIEW AND CONSULTATION

During your customized bridal preview, Karan will work directly with you to determine which hair and makeup look will fit you and your wedding party best. Based on any photos that have caught your eye, your personal style, best features and overall wedding day look, she will then design the perfect wedding day look for each bride... Whether that will be a very soft, natural look, or full on glam! Previews are suggested to be done about 4-6 weeks before the wedding for the best results.
